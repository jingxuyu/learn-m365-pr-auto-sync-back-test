
In your multi-national organization, you had a successful deployment of Microsoft Teams and you wanted to investigate the tools you could use to manage users and devices. You also needed a way to monitor usage and call quality. The Teams admin portal is a central location for you to manage the Teams environment for your users. It provides policies of several types, monitoring tools, and other configuration settings that address all these needs.

Now you've completed this module, you should be able to:

- Explain how to configure user access levels to Teams in your organization.
- Demonstrate how to use policies to manage Teams users, apps, meetings, and messaging.
- Describe how to manage the Apps that are available to your Teams users.
- Explain how to set up voice features in Teams.
- Describe how to manage the mobile devices that use Teams.
- Describe how to analyze users' behavior in Teams.
