## Multiple Choice
What is MyAnalytics?
( ) The ability automatically sort your email {{Incorrect. Outlook includes many intelligent ways of sorting email, but MyAnalytics does not sort your email.}}

## Multiple Choice
What is the main benefit of Workplace Analytics?
