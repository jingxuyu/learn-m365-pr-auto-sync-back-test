In your organization, you were aware of the many diverse attacks that hackers use to penetrate your systems and of the difficulty of staying up to date with the latest potential problems. You wanted to take an active approach to defense by minimizing the attack surface of your endpoint devices. By using attack surface reduction rules, you were able to close many of these vulnerabilities.
